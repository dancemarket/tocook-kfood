## Kijogae-Namul
### Seasoned Pen Shell Greens · 키조개나물무침

Korean cuisine is all about harmony, and **Kijogae** is a perfect example. The earthy tones of Pen Shell create a comforting meal for any day of the week.

In Korea, the Namul method has been perfected over generations to bring out the natural sweetness of ingredients like Pen Shell. When you make **Kijogae**, you aren't just cooking; you're participating in a rich culinary heritage.

### 🐚 Kijogae (Pen Shell · 키조개)

This recipes highlights **Kijogae**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea.

### Cooking Tips for Kijogae
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Pen Shell that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.
