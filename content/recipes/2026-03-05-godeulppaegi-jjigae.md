## Godeulppaegi-Jjigae
### Korean Lettuce Korean Stew · 고들빼기찌개

If you're looking for an authentic taste of Korea, look no further than **Godeulppaegi**. This recipe features seasonal Korean Lettuce at its absolute peak of flavor.

This dish is a staple in many Korean households because of its simplicity and health benefits. Korean Lettuce is known for being rich in vitamins, and preparing it as **Godeulppaegi** is the traditional way to enjoy its full profile.
