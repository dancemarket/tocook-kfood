## Ttalgi-Namul
### Seasoned Strawberry Greens · 딸기나물무침

Korean cuisine is all about harmony, and **Ttalgi** is a perfect example. The earthy tones of Strawberry create a comforting meal for any day of the week.

This dish is a staple in many Korean households because of its simplicity and health benefits. Strawberry is known for being rich in vitamins, and preparing it as **Ttalgi** is the traditional way to enjoy its full profile.

### 🍓 Ttalgi (Strawberry · 딸기)

This recipes highlights **Ttalgi**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea. 

---

### Cooking Tips for Ttalgi
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Strawberry that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.

Enjoy your healthy, homemade Korean meal!
