## Eomnamu-sun-Bokkeum
### Stir-fried Kalopanax Shoots · 엄나무순볶음

Welcome back! Today's seasonal spotlight is on Kalopanax Shoots, the star of our delicious **Eomnamu-sun** recipe. It's simple, healthy, and packed with nutrients.

The beauty of Korean cuisine lies in 'Yak-sik-dong-won' — the belief that food and medicine share the same source. By using Bokkeum with fresh Kalopanax Shoots, this **Eomnamu-sun** becomes a nourishing treat for the soul.
