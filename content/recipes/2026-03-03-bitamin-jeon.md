## Bitamin-Jeon
### Vitamin Greens Savory Pancake · 비타민전

Many people ask what makes Korean food so special. The answer is often simple, fresh ingredients like Vitamin Greens transformed into a delicious **Bitamin**.

The beauty of Korean cuisine lies in 'Yak-sik-dong-won' — the belief that food and medicine share the same source. By using Jeon with fresh Vitamin Greens, this **Bitamin** becomes a nourishing treat for the soul.

### 🌿 Bitamin (Vitamin Greens · 비타민)

This recipes highlights **Bitamin**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea.

### Cooking Tips for Bitamin
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Vitamin Greens that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.
