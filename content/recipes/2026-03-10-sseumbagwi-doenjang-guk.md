## Sseumbagwi-Doenjangguk
### Ixeris Soybean Paste Soup · 씀바귀된장국

Today, we are exploring a beloved classic that defines Korean home cooking: **Sseumbagwi**. Using fresh Ixeris is the key to mastering this dish.

The beauty of Korean cuisine lies in 'Yak-sik-dong-won' — the belief that food and medicine share the same source. By using Doenjangguk with fresh Ixeris, this **Sseumbagwi** becomes a nourishing treat for the soul.

### 🌿 Sseumbagwi (Ixeris · 씀바귀)

This recipes highlights **Sseumbagwi**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea. 

---

### Cooking Tips for Sseumbagwi
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Ixeris that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.

Enjoy your healthy, homemade Korean meal!
