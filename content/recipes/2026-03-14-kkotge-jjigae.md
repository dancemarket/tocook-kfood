## Kkotge-Jjigae
### Blue Crab Korean Stew · 꽃게찌개

If you're looking for an authentic taste of Korea, look no further than **Kkotge**. This recipe features seasonal Blue Crab at its absolute peak of flavor.

The beauty of Korean cuisine lies in 'Yak-sik-dong-won' — the belief that food and medicine share the same source. By using Jjigae with fresh Blue Crab, this **Kkotge** becomes a nourishing treat for the soul.

### 🦀 Kkotge (Blue Crab · 꽃게)

This recipes highlights **Kkotge**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea.

### Cooking Tips for Kkotge
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Blue Crab that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.

Enjoy your healthy, homemade Korean meal!
