## Dodari-Jeon
### Flounder Savory Pancake · 도다리전

Many people ask what makes Korean food so special. The answer is often simple, fresh ingredients like Flounder transformed into a delicious **Dodari**.

This dish is a staple in many Korean households because of its simplicity and health benefits. Flounder is known for being rich in vitamins, and preparing it as **Dodari** is the traditional way to enjoy its full profile.

### 🐟 Dodari (Flounder · 도다리)

This recipes highlights **Dodari**, which is currently in its prime during the **Spring** season in Korea. 

---

### Cooking Tips for Dodari
*   **Freshness:** Always look for Flounder that has a firm texture.
*   **Balance:** Remember that Korean cooking is about balancing the five flavors.
*   **Serving:** This dish pairs perfectly with a bowl of warm multigrain rice.
